I am a Data Scientist/Analyst with 3 years of experience in analyzing and interpreting large datasets to uncover insights and improve business performance. While writing my thesis for my psychology bachelor’s degree I got curious of data and programming, and decided to start a master’s degree in Behavioural Data Science. I am passionate about using data to drive decision-making and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.
People often describe me as a social and outgoing person, but I am also a very curious person and I enjoy learning as much as I can. I like working in teams but I am also able to work independently.

Experience

Since Dec 2021

Data Analyst - Stuvia

Amsterdam, NL

At Stuvia, an online marketplace which students can use to sell their summaries and notes, I am the responsible data analyst. I do analysis in the fields of marketing, finance and user experience. We track user behaviour and use it to optimize functionalities on the website.

  • My first large project was the reconstruction of formulas and weights of the search function behind our main search bar, which resulted in more intuitive search results.
  • I set up a data warehouse in AWS Redshift from our live MySQL data using AWS Glue, thereby getting familiar with the intricacies of data engineering using an ETL.

May 2021 - Sep 2021

Intern Data Science - DPG Media

Amsterdam, NL

At the biggest media company of the Benelux I was a part of the News Analytics Team. DPG Media is a large company and has complex data structures.

  • My main project was an analysis on the success of an article by generating and modelling contextual variables. This model was then integrated into the company’s processes.
  • I learned how to visualize using Looker and got more familiar with data wrangling and notebooks.

Feb 2020 - Jul 2020

Intern Data Analysis - Prowise Learn

Amsterdam, NL

Prowise Learn, formerly called “Oefenweb”, is a software company with an application that contains adaptive online exercises for primary and secondary education. This provided me with a great place for improving my psychometric skills.

  • At Prowise Learn, I worked on the adaptive scoring system that they used to evaluate several different scoring values of the user by psychometrically analyzing and evaluating them.
  • I got even more familiar with how to thoroughly analyze and esthetically visualize large sets of data in R.
