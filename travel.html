Travel

Since I was young, I had the luck of having parents that were wealthy enough to bring me to many
beautiful places around the world. Together with my mother, my father and my sister, I visited mainly warm and comfortable spots.
Although that's still my favorite type of destination, I have developed a more wide interest in traveling in my adolescent years. After my exchange in Budapest I started to travel a lot on my own, visiting friends or hopping hostels in a foreign place. In these years I've been on amazing trips, but I've also experienced how things can go wrong. I learned a lot more about different types of tourism and what type I like the best myself.
Now the places I visited in the world are set out in Google Maps left of here.
