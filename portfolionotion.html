About Me

I am a data scientist/analyst with 3 years of experience in analyzing and interpreting large datasets to uncover insights and improve business performance. While writing my thesis for my psychology bachelor’s degree I got curious of data and programming, and decided to start a master’s degree in Behavioural Data Science. I am passionate about using data to drive decision-making and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.
People often describe me as a social and outgoing person, but I am also a very curious person and I enjoy learning as much as I can. I like working in teams but I am also able to work independently.
