Music

My musical interests are wide. Over the years they have thoroughly developed and therefore I am now
widely interest in both historic and modern music. For that reason I decided to create a topic to display some of my most favorite artists. When I was younger I started listening to a lot of Old School Hiphop like 2Pac and the Notorious BIG. I listened to lots of pop music in those years, but now I am more interested in classical segments of music. One of my favorite genres when I feel blue is flamenco, so artists like Paco De Lucia, Cameron de la Isla and Vicente Amigo really speak to me. Another beautiful and wide genre is jazz. I mainly like bossa nova jazz and classical jazz, although John Coltrane interests me as well.
